Offline-First Sync

Commands you send on spotty wifi never get lost.

The part that makes this actually work: offline-first. Commands you send while on spotty wifi or in the subway get queued locally. When you reconnect, Background Sync replays everything automatically — even if the tab is closed. Three-layer persistence (Service Worker cache, localStorage, IndexedDB) means you never lose state. You're never out of the loop.

  • Commands queued in IndexedDB when offline or on spotty wifi
  • Background Sync replays queue automatically on reconnect
  • Works even if the tab was closed — no lost commands
  • Three-layer persistence: Service Worker → localStorage → IndexedDB
